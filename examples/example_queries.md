# Example Queries for AI Data Analyst

This file contains example queries you can use with the sample sales data.

## Basic Statistics

- "Show me summary statistics for all columns"
- "What's the average revenue per product?"
- "How many sales were made in total?"

## Top/Bottom Analysis

- "What are the top 5 products by revenue?"
- "Which region has the lowest sales?"
- "Show me the bottom 3 performing months"

## Trend Analysis

- "Show me sales trends over time"
- "How did revenue change month by month?"
- "Plot revenue trends for each product"
- "Show monthly sales comparison"

## Regional Analysis

- "Compare sales performance across regions"
- "Which region has the highest revenue?"
- "Show me revenue distribution by region"
- "How do different regions compare in terms of sales?"

## Product Analysis

- "Compare all products by revenue"
- "Which product has the most sales?"
- "Show me product performance over time"
- "What's the average price per product?"

## Time-based Questions

- "Why did sales drop in July?"
- "What happened in December?"
- "Compare Q1 to Q4"
- "Show me seasonal trends"

## Correlations

- "Is there a correlation between sales and revenue?"
- "Show me the relationship between product and region performance"
- "Find patterns in the data"

## Complex Queries

- "Which product-region combination performs best?"
- "Calculate the growth rate for each product"
- "Find anomalies in the sales data"
- "Predict next month's revenue based on trends"

## Data Quality

- "Are there any null values in the data?"
- "Show me data quality issues"
- "Check for duplicate entries"
- "Validate data types"

## Custom Calculations

- "Calculate the revenue per sale for each product"
- "What's the market share of each region?"
- "Show me year-over-year growth"
- "Calculate moving averages for sales"

## Try These Follow-up Questions

After getting an initial answer, ask follow-ups like:

- "Can you show that as a chart?"
- "Why is that the case?"
- "What about other products?"
- "Compare that to the previous period"
- "Show me more details"

The AI agent maintains conversation context, so it remembers what you asked before!
